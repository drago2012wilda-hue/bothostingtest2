Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how ItzWilda (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects personal data through https://dcbothoster.onrender.com (“BotHoster”).

1. Information We Collect

  • Discord Account Data: When you log in, we receive your Discord user ID, username, avatar, and discriminator.
  • Bot Data: When you create a bot, we store its encrypted token, language, and configuration.
  • Billing Data: Stripe handles all payment processing; we do not store credit card information.
  • Logs and Diagnostics: We collect limited technical data (e.g., IP address, errors, system logs) to ensure reliability.
  • IP Address: We collect IP addresses to restrict the use of VPNs or related services.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the BotHoster platform.
  • To authenticate you via Discord OAuth.
  • To manage bot hosting and user subscriptions.
  • To comply with legal obligations or enforce our Terms of Service.

3. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell or rent your personal data.
  • We share limited information with:
    • Discord, Inc. for authentication.
    • Stripe, Inc. for payment processing.
    • Supabase for database and storage infrastructure.

4. Data Retention

We retain data as long as your account remains active. You can request deletion by contacting dcbothoster@gmail.com.

5. Security

We use AES-256 encryption for sensitive fields (e.g., bot tokens). While we strive for security, no system is entirely risk-free.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our service is for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. International Data

BotHoster operates in Canada. Your data may be processed outside your country of residence.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The latest version will always be posted on this page.

9. Contact

For questions, contact us at dcbothoster@gmail.com.
